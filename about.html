About This Website

This website of inspirational quotes was made for the BYU-Idaho Elevate project. This was designed as part of a pilot program for the including of the Technical Teamwork class sections as part of the Elevate program. This website has been designed to try and help people cultivate peace and courage in turbulent times. We hope that this website will inspire people and their inspiration will lead to overcoming fears and social change.


About BYU-Idaho

Brigham Young University Idaho is located in Rexburg, Idaho. Previously known as Ricks College in 2001 it was changed to a four-year institution and was renamed Brigham Young University Idaho. The university is sponsored by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints


About Elevate

The ELEVATE project is designed to help inspire students to find solutions to social problems and provide a chance to make a difference.
